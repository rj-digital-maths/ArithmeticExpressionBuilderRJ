## 📖 How to Use the App (Step-by-Step)

### 1️⃣ Setting Up the Quiz
* **Student Name & Class:** Type in the student's name and their current class grade.
* **Difficulty Level:** Choose between **Easy** (numbers 1-10), **Medium** (numbers 1-20), or **Hard** (numbers 1-50).
* **License Key (Optional):** * If left **blank**, the app runs in **Free Trial Mode** (fixed to 3 questions, performance PDF summary is locked).
  * Enter your purchased **Premium Key** to unlock the custom question length slider (5 to 25 questions) and full PDF downloads.

### 2️⃣ Playing the Quiz
* Look at the **Target Number** displayed on the left side of a question.
* Look at the **Three Blue Number Boxes** displayed on the right side.
* Use **all three numbers** along with basic operators like addition (`+`), subtraction (`-`), or parentheses `()` to write a mathematical expression string that calculates exactly to the Target Number.
* *Example:* If your target is `5` and your numbers are `2, 4, 1`, your input expression could be `4 + 2 - 1` or `(4 - 1) + 2`.

### 3️⃣ Submitting & Getting Results
* Click the primary **Submit Quiz** button at the bottom.
* Watch the colorful balloons celebrate your score!
* If using a Premium Key, click **Save Result as PDF** to instantly download a beautifully formatted progress report card for homework tracking.
